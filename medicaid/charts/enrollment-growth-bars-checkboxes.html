Enrollment Growth Using Checkboxes to Show/Hide Series

The chart below displays the percentage growth in total Medicaid and CHIP enrollment as compared to the period prior to the start of first Marketplace open enrollment period (July-Sept. 2013) for states that reported relevant data for both periods. The enrollment figures are point-in-time counts (as of the last day of the month) and include only enrollees who received comprehensive benefit packages.
